About Me

Hello! My name is Markus (he/him) and I grew up in the old but charming city of Mainz, Germany (in fact, its history dates back to 12 BC!) This is where I spent my entire childhood together with my two sisters. Being German, this meant playing lots of soccer. At the age of 8, my father got me my first personal computer: a chunky tower running Windows 95. Looking back, this is when my love for technology started.

Next to Germany, I have lived in Sweden and Ireland before settling in beautiful Vancouver, Canada which I call home now. It was in Vancouver where I discovered my calling for the outdoors: summers are spent hiking, camping, backpacking, biking and surfing. In winter I switch to snowshoeing and snowboarding. Running and bouldering are two of my favourite activities I do all year round with my partner.

My Experience

Experience

After finishing my Master's degree in computer science, I started my professional career in the field of data science. Back in Germany, I was part of a research and development team that developed a patented rapid diagnostic test for glaucoma. In this group, I took on the role of the data scientist and developed a machine learning system able to detect glaucoma based on antigens extracted from teardrops.

After a jump across the pond, I continued working in the data field in Vancouver, Canada. As a data analyst, I focused my efforts on geographic information systems and developed tooling to process, transform and standardize geospatial data on a large scale. Later on, I continued to push forward this new product area I established by switching careers to product management and owning the so-called Location Services Platform, which contained a number of location awareness features to power millions of customer experiences globally.

Some time later, I grew into the role of a Senior Product Manager and was responsible for the two product areas Analytics and Data Platform at a tech startup with series B funding in the renewable energy space. There, I partnered with software engineers, data scientists and designers to continuously iterate on the product and to launch new features. I took up my next challenge not too long after and headed up the product team as Director of Product where I scaled and matured the product organization and set out product direction and business strategy.

Fast-forward to today, I shifted my focus to software development and want to continue creating great products using my experience, technology mindset and coding skills.
