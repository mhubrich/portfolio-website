About Me

Hello! My name is Markus and I grew up in the old but charming city of Mainz, Germany (in fact, its history dates back to 12 BC!) This is where I spent my entire childhood together with my two sisters. Being German, this meant playing lots of soccer. At the age of 8, my father got me my first personal computer: a chunky tower running Windows 95. Looking back, this is when my love for technology started.

Next to Germany, I have lived in Sweden and Ireland before settling in beautiful Vancouver, Canada which I call home now. It was in Vancouver where I discovered my calling for the outdoors: summers are spent hiking, camping, backpacking, biking and surfing. In winter I switch to snowshoeing and snowboarding. Running and bouldering are two of my favourite activities I do all year round with my partner.
